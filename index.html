Size Guide

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape and measure over bare skin or thin clothing for the most accurate fit.

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips
Size Bust (inches) Waist (inches) Hips (inches)
XS 30-32 23-25 33-35
S 32-34 25-27 35-37
M 34-36 27-29 37-39
L 36-38 29-31 39-41
XL 38-40 31-33 41-43

Tip: Our swimsuits are designed to fit snugly. If you're between sizes or prefer more coverage, we recommend sizing up!
