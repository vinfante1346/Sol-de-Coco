Brand Voice & Tone

How we talk to our babes

✓ DO
  • Use lowercase, casual vibes
  • Be authentic & relatable
  • Use emojis (☀️🌴🌊💗)
  • Keep it fun & playful
  • Show personality
  • Use "babes", "girls", "you"
✗ DON'T
  • Be corporate or formal
  • Overuse exclamation marks
  • Sound salesy or pushy
  • Use jargon or complex words
  • Be too serious
  • Sound fake or forced

Examples:

✓ "summer's calling and she wants you in this bikini ☀️"

✓ "just dropped: your new fav swimsuit"

✓ "made with love for beach babes like you 🌴"

Influencer Strategy

Micro-influencers for authentic growth

Target Influencers

  • 3K-30K followers
  • Engagement rate >5%
  • Beach/travel/lifestyle content
  • Audience: women 18-35
  • USA based (coastal states)

Partnership Model

  • Month 1-2: Gifting only
  • Month 3+: $50-200 per post
  • Unique discount codes (15-20%)
  • Affiliate commission (10%)
  • Long-term brand ambassadors

Monthly Goals

Month 1-2: 10 influencers (gifting) • Month 3-4: 15 influencers (5 paid) • Month 5-6: 20 influencers (10 paid)
